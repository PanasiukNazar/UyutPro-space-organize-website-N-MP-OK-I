Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal data when you visit our website or purchase our investment education services. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your data.

1. General Provisions

We value the protection of your personal data and process it confidentially and in compliance with data protection laws. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have concerning your data.

2. Information Collection

We collect various types of information to provide you with better service:

2.1 Personal Data

When you visit our website or purchase our investment education services, we may collect personal data such as your name, address, email, and payment information. This data is necessary for processing your orders and providing our services.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect information about your computer, including your IP address, the browser used, and access time. This data helps us analyze and improve the use of our website.

3. Use of Your Data

We use the collected data for various purposes, including:

  • Processing and managing your orders;
  • Providing you with information about our investment education services;
  • Improving our customer service;
  • Analyzing and optimizing the use of our website;
  • Sending marketing materials to you if you have consented to this.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent, except where necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations or to comply with legal requirements. Possible recipients of your data include:

  • Delivery services that fulfill your orders;
  • Payment services for processing payments;
  • IT service companies that help us manage the website.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site. They help us collect information about how you use the site and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site features may become unavailable.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This includes data encryption, firewalls, and regular security audits.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about your personal data that we store at any time. Additionally, you have the right to:

  • Request correction of incorrect data;
  • Request the deletion of your personal data;
  • Request restriction of data processing;
  • Object to the processing of your personal data;
  • Request data portability.

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the contact details on our website.

8. Links to Third-Party Resources

Our website may contain links to third-party websites operated by other parties. We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of these websites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these websites before submitting any personal information.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately upon publication. We recommend checking this policy regularly to stay informed about any updates.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or our data protection practices, please contact us through the contact page on our website.

Thank you for visiting our website. Your trust is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting and respecting your personal data.
